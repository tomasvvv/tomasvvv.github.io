# Privacy Policy

**App:** Tidy Roll
**Developer:** tomasvvv
**Effective date:** 2026-06-01

Tidy Roll ("the app", "we", "us") is a photo and video cleanup tool. Your privacy
matters, and the design is simple: **your photos and videos stay on your device,
and we collect only limited anonymous analytics metadata to understand and improve
the app.**

## The short version

- We do **not** collect personal information.
- We do **not** have servers, accounts, or logins.
- We use privacy-friendly analytics to collect anonymous app usage metadata.
- We do **not** use advertising, tracking, or data brokers.
- Your photos and videos **never leave your device** and are never uploaded to us
  or anyone else.

## Photo library access

Tidy Roll requests access to your photo library so it can show your photos and
videos and let you keep or delete them. This access is used **only on your device**
to display the media you choose to review and to carry out deletions you confirm.
We never copy, transmit, or retain your media. You can grant full or limited access
and change or revoke it at any time in the iOS **Settings → Privacy & Security →
Photos**.

## Anonymous analytics

Tidy Roll uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to collect
anonymous metadata about how the app is used. This helps us understand whether the
app is working well, which features are useful, and where users may run into
problems.

The analytics we collect may include events such as app launches, onboarding
completion, permission status, daily cleanup completion, streak or milestone
activity, paywall views, purchase or restore results, and high-level deletion
counts or storage amounts. We may also receive basic technical metadata such as
app version, device type, operating system version, language, region, session
information, crash data, and performance data.

We do **not** send your photos, videos, filenames, photo contents, location data,
contacts, advertising identifiers, email address, name, or payment-card details to
TelemetryDeck. Analytics events are not linked to an account, because Tidy Roll has
no accounts or logins.

TelemetryDeck may use an anonymous installation or device/vendor identifier to
count unique users and sessions. This identifier is used for analytics only, is not
used for advertising, and is not used to track you across apps or websites.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of information locally on your device (using the
system's standard on-device storage) to make the app work — for example your review
progress, streaks, settings, and daily limits. This data stays on your device, is
not uploaded as personal data, and is removed when you delete the app.

## Purchases

Tidy Roll Pro is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase system as auto-renewing
Monthly and Yearly subscriptions and a one-time Lifetime purchase. **Apple processes
all payments.** We never receive or store your payment-card details. Apple's handling
of purchase data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. We may collect anonymous
analytics events about purchase flow results, such as whether a purchase was
started, completed, failed, restored, renewed, cancelled, upgraded, or downgraded.

## Children

Tidy Roll is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any
information from children.

## Data retention and your rights

Most app data lives on your device and is under your control; deleting the app
removes it from your device. Anonymous analytics metadata may be retained by
TelemetryDeck so we can understand app usage over time. Because this analytics data
is not tied to an account or direct personal identifier, we generally cannot look
up, export, or delete analytics records for a specific person.
