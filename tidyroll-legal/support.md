# Tidy Roll — Support

Tidy Roll helps you swipe through your photos and videos to quickly keep or delete
them and free up storage — all on your device.

**Need help? Email us at [tidyrollapp@gmail.com](mailto:tidyrollapp@gmail.com).** We're
happy to help.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I restore my purchase?
Open **Settings** inside the app and tap **Restore Purchases**. This re-syncs your
Tidy Roll Pro purchase with your Apple account. Make sure you're signed in with the
same Apple ID you used to buy.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not inside the app. On your device go to
**Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions**, choose **Tidy Roll**, and tap **Cancel
Subscription**. You'll keep Pro until the end of the current billing period. (The
Lifetime option is a one-time purchase and has nothing to cancel.)

### What happens to photos I delete?
Tidy Roll uses the standard iOS deletion flow: deleted items move to your **Recently
Deleted** album in the Photos app and are permanently removed after the system
retention period. You can recover them from Recently Deleted until then. Please keep
your own backups.

### Tidy Roll only sees some of my photos
That means the app has **limited** photo access. To let it see your whole library, go
to **Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Tidy Roll** and choose **Full Access**.
You can also tap **Select more** in the app to add photos to the limited selection.

### Is my data private?
Yes. Tidy Roll has no accounts or servers, collects no personal data, and your photos
never leave your device. See our [Privacy Policy](https://tomasvvv.github.io/tidyroll-legal/privacy/).
